Content Rules — Fortis

All content must:

- be practical and applicable
- be based on real work situations
- teach something useful
- correct unsafe behavior
- generate awareness of risk

Avoid:

- generic content
- obvious statements
- repeated ideas
- motivational empty phrases
- long explanations without application

Structure:

- strong hook
- clear progression
- practical explanation
- clear outcome

If the content does not teach, it is incorrect.s
